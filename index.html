SELF CATERING STUDENTS

  • You must be a UCT Registered Student staying in a UCT Residence in Western Cape
  • Students staying in self-catering residences needs to earn a minimum of R2 000 per month
  • Students must produce a 6 months bank statement with a consistent Amount of R2000 or above
  • Students must Produce South African ID document and consent to be verified
  • Students must produce a proof of registration & proof of UCT residence from the university
  • Students must sign a contract, acknowledging and accepting all the Nkosi Contributions financial procedures, terms and conditions…Etc.
